Precious Heritage School is a model co-educational school located in Basorun area in Ibadan. We offer high quality standard education and inculcate Godly discipline as we take children through our Crèche, Playgroup, Pre-nursery, Nursery, Primary and Secondary classes.

At Precious Heritage School, we believe that every child should have access to a Godly educational background which will help to prepare them for a successful future.

The convenience of having classes ranging from Crèche till Secondary level affords Parents and children the stability needed to progress academically with ease.

OUR HISTORY

Precious Heritage School was founded in July 2004, when it began as a Crèche, giving foundational education to children. Ever since, the school has grown into a high flying institution that caters for academic excellence in all her students.

We started with the Crèche at our first location, a four-bedroom apartment, where we spent three years. Progressive growth over the years has brought the school to her permanent site located strategically on the border of four Local Government Areas in Oyo State, making it easily accessible to parents and offering a serene and quiet place of learning. From an initial population of 3 pupils, the school has witnessed tremendous growth to a current population of over 400 pupils.

We celebrate our history with delight as much as we eagerly anticipate our advancement as an institution rising to fulfill the educational standards of the 21st Century.
